About Me

I am now an assistant researcher in Tsinghua University from 2021.3.
I finished my PhD (advisor: Prof. Shi-Min Hu) in Tsinghua University in 2018.7, and my bachelor in Xidian University in 2013.7.
I worked as a postdoc researcher in Tsinghua University from 2018.9 to 2021.2.
